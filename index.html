How this calculator works

This calculator estimates maintenance energy requirements for large reptiles (monitor lizards and snakes) using metabolic scaling principles commonly applied in herpetology and zoo nutrition.

  • Metabolic scaling: Basal energy requirement is estimated using body mass raised to the power of 0.75 (BM0.75).
  • Temperature adjustment: Energy demand is adjusted using a Q10 of 2 with 30 °C as reference.
  • Feeding conversion: Weekly energy requirements are converted to prey mass using average prey energy values.
